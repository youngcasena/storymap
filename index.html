Global Distribution of Lions

Lions are one of the most iconic and majestic creatures on Earth, known as the "King of the Jungle." Historically, lions were found throughout Africa, Asia, and Europe. However, their populations have drastically declined due to habitat loss, human-wildlife conflict, and poaching. Today, lions primarily inhabit the savannas of sub-Saharan Africa, with a small population of Asiatic lions surviving in the Gir Forest of India.

This map highlights some of the key regions where lions are found, showcasing the varying population sizes and the unique characteristics of each habitat. From the Maasai Mara in Kenya to the Gir Forest in India, each location plays a crucial role in the conservation of these magnificent animals.
